Questions & Answers
Feel free to contact us if you have any more questions
How do I start?
Simply contact us, and we will gladly provide you all the details relative to the agreement and revenue model. We will then provide you a simple agreement, short documentation and marketing material, as well as a unique promo code.
How do I make money?
You can make additional revenues by selling the integration of booxi to your clients, and/or by selling booxi membership. You can also decide to offer free months of booxi to your client as added value. Each time you register a new client using your unique promo code we can track your clients and return you some revenues.
What are the fees?
The affiliate program is free and booxi will only charge your clients for the booxi membership and associated usage fees. booxi does not take any part of your revenues for the services you offer to your clients. You are free to charge for the integration of booxi and keep the revenues associated with your services.
Am I eligible to the affiliate program?
If you’re business is to offer web design and development services to small business, you can be part of this program no matter the size of your organization including self-employed developers. If you do not plan on having more than one project, you do not need this program, use the developer section directly.
